The Plaza Athenee

The Hotel Plaza Athenee is a Brunei-owned history luxury hotel in Paris, France. It is located at 25 Avenue Montaihne in the 8th arrondissement of Paris, near the Champs-Elysees and the Palais de Tokyo. The hotel is part of the Dorchester Collection group of international luxury hotels. The hotel has five restaurants and a bar, and it has room rates ranging from US$1,254 to US$20,000 per night for the hotel's premier suite.
